Minimum Degree algorithm

Based on the article: The Minimum Degree Heuristic and the Minimal Triangulation Process by A. Berry, Pinar Heggernes & Geneviève Simonet.

Author(s): Matthieu SozeauPierre-Loic Garoche
module P : 
functor (G : Sig.P) -> sig .. end
type edgeset = (G.V.t * G.V.t) list
val md : G.t -> G.t * edgeset * G.V.t list
md g return a tuple (g', e, o) where g' is a triangulated graph, e is the triangulation of g and o is a perfect elimination order of g'
val triangulate : G.t -> G.t
triangulate g return the graph g' produced by applying miminum degree to g.
